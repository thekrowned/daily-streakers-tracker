Full Streakers
Full streakers are those who never break their streaks since day one which is really cool and should be appreciated for their hard work.
    Casual Streakers
    Casual streakers are those who broke their streaks at one point but are still dedicated to playing the daily challenge which is also really cool.
      Not Streaking
